About

I am a graduate student in the PDOL lab advised by Prof. Gary Wang. I work on data- and physics-informed generative models for engineering design, including conditional DDPM/RePaint for parametric hull design, graph diffusion for mesh and beam structures, and tabular generative modeling for design optimization.

Research Interests

Generative Design

Diffusion models (DDPM, SDE, flow matching) and conditional guidance (RePaint, feasibility, performance predictors) for parameterized engineering designs.

Graph/mesh modeling

Graph diffusion, GNNs, and image-to-graph meshing pipelines for structure synthesis and topology optimization.
