Ke Wang

I am a Graduate Researcher at the Product Design & Optimization Lab (PDOL), Simon Fraser University, where I am supervised by Prof. Gary Wang. I received my Bachelor's degree in Mechatronic Systems Engineering from Simon Fraser University. My research focuses on AI-driven generative design, diffusion models, and graph-based modeling for engineering design optimization.

I am passionate about integrating machine learning with physics-based design processes to accelerate innovation in design generation and optimization. My work explores conditional diffusion models, graph neural networks, and foundation model for performance-guided design synthesis.
